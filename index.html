Thank You

We would like to thank everyone who contributed to this year’s Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop (SBW). A special thanks to our speakers for sharing their expertise, our sponsors for making the event possible, and all attendees for the lively discussions.

Introduction to SBW2026

The Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop (SBW) is an annual event organised by different Swedish universities since 2000, and is the largest gathering of bioinformaticians and computational biologists in Sweden. The meeting welcomes participants from all career stages and sectors, with a strong focus on PhD students and postdocs.

The 2026 edition took place in Gothenburg at the Wallenberg Conference Center on March 12 and 13, 2026. The programme featured keynote presentations, contributed talks, hands-on workshops, and a poster session, with national and international speakers. SBW2026 aligns with the Nordic Computational Biology week.

Programme at a glance

  • Two full days of talks, six themed sessions, and a dedicated poster session
  • Four hands-on workshops: Data management, Academic Writing, Data Visualisation, Bacterial Genomics
  • Networking opportunities with national and international keynote speakers

Session topics

  • Translational research
  • Multi-omics data integration
  • Single-cell technologies
  • Cancer genomics
  • Machine learning and AI
  • Metagenomics and microbiome

Early‑career opportunities

SBW strongly encourages PhD students and postdocs to submit abstracts for contributed talks and posters.
