Welcome

Welcome to the Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop (SBW) 2024!
The largest bioinformatics and computational biology conference in Sweden will be hosted in Uppsala, at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences, on November 4 and 5, 2024.

Introduction to SBW2024

The Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop (SBW) is an annual event organized by different universities in Sweden since 2000. It is the largest gathering of bioinformaticians and computational biologists in Sweden.

This year, we are happy to announce that the event will take place in Uppsala, on November 4-5 at The Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences. The event is targeted at and organized by PhD students and postdocs, but it's open to everyone working with any kind of bioinformatics or computational biology.

Senior scientists and non-academic participants are welcome. Additionally, the SBW2024 is part of the Nordic Computational Biology (Nordic CompBio) week and is open to participants from other Nordic countries. Visit Nordic CompBio for more information and updates on regional programs.

SBW 2025 organizing committee - The SBW is held in a different Swedish city every year and traditionally organized by a team of PhD students and Postdocs. If you are interested in being involved in the organization of the SBW 2025, please reach out to us at info@sbw2024.org.

No-show fees

Participation in the SBW is free of charge for students and postdocs at Swedish Universities. However, if you register as an on-site participant and do not attend the event (no-show), your institution will be invoiced 1,000 SEK. Other academic and non-academic participants will be invoiced the registration fee. Please provide a valid invoice address upon registration, including reference codes to the paying project. Cancellation via email to info@sbw2024.org is free until October 21, 2024.

Please note that the SBW cannot invoice individuals.
